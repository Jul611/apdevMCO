Computer Lab Rules

Welcome Lab Rat! To ensure a safe and productive environment for everyone, please follow these rules:

DISCLAIMER: We reserve the right to deny access or remove users from the computer lab for violation of these rules or any other inappropriate behavior

General Use and Safety

  • No food or drinks are allowed near the computers.
  • Keep personal belongings organized and off the floor.
  • Don't move, unplug, or reconfigure any computer components without permission.
  • Don't install unauthorized software or tamper with system settings.
  • Report any electrical problems, damaged equipment, or safety hazards to the lab supervisor immediately.

Etiquette and Respect

  • Maintain a quiet environment for others working in the lab. Use headphones for listening to music or videos.
  • Don't interfere with other users' computers, files, or projects.
  • Print responsibly and avoid excessive printing.
  • Be mindful of time limits, especially if there's high demand for computers.
  • Log out properly when finished and close all programs you were using.

Security and Data Protection

  • Don't access unauthorized accounts or personal information.
  • Be responsible with your online activity and avoid illegal or inappropriate content.
  • Only download files from trusted sources.
  • Scan external drives for viruses before using them in the lab computers.
  • Be cautious of phishing scams and don't enter personal information on suspicious websites.

Additional Rules

  • Mobile phones: Silent mode or turn them off if disruptive.
  • Pets: Not allowed in the computer lab.
  • Cheating: Using the lab for unauthorized activities like test cheating is strictly prohibited.
  • Follow instructions: Always abide by any specific instructions provided by the lab supervisor or instructor.
